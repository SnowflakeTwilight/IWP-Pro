Witness the awe-inspiring scenery as you behold breathtaking vistas that captivate the senses. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature's grandeur and savor the moments of tranquility and wonder.

Narsobachi Wadi

Hidden Oasis: Nestled amidst the bustling cityscape of Pune, Narsobachi Wadi is a verdant sanctuary offering a welcome escape from the urban jungle. Explore its lush gardens, discover ancient temples, and unwind beside the serene waters of its lake.

Historical Enchantment: This sprawling estate, dating back to the 18th century, holds whispers of the Peshwa era. Immerse yourself in its rich history, admire the architectural marvels like the Narsobachi Wada, and delve into the stories of its past inhabitants.

Cultural Tapestry: Experience the vibrant cultural melange of Maharashtra at Narsobachi Wadi. Witness traditional folk performances, participate in vibrant festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi, and savor the authentic flavors of regional cuisine.

Bawanthwadi Bird Santuary

Feathered Paradise: This haven for avifauna boasts over 270 species of birds, from vibrant flamingos and graceful pelicans to elusive owls and playful parakeets. Birdwatchers will delight in the symphony of calls, the kaleidoscope of colors, and the opportunity to observe these feathered wonders in their natural habitat.

Mangrove Magic: Bawanthwadi's unique ecosystem of mangroves, lagoons, and mudflats provides a vital breeding ground and feeding area for various bird species. Learn about the delicate balance of this ecosystem, its role in coastal protection, and the fascinating adaptations of its winged inhabitants.

Peaceful Escape: Find tranquility amidst the rustling leaves and serene waterways of Bawanthwadi. Take a boat ride through the mangroves, explore the nearby villages, and soak in the serenity of this protected haven for both birds and humans.

Krishna River

Life-Giving Lifeline: Flowing through the length of Maharashtra, the Krishna River is more than just a body of water; it's the lifeblood of the region. Witness farmers nurturing their lands along its banks, fishers casting their nets, and communities thriving on its bounty.

Spiritual Serenity: The river holds immense spiritual significance, with several pilgrimage sites dotting its banks. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of Mahabaleshwar Temple, witness the grand Kumbh Mela festival, and find solace in the ancient rituals connected to this sacred river.

Adventure Playground: Embark on thrilling white-water rafting adventures along the rapids of the Krishna River. Kayak through calm stretches, spot diverse birdlife, and experience the raw beauty of nature's power unleashed.
