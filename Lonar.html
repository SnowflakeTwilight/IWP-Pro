Lonar Crater lake

Cosmic Kiss: This 6 km wide, sapphire-hued lake is believed to be formed by a meteor impact 50,000 years ago, making it one of the only hypervelocity impact craters in basaltic rock on Earth. Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of this geological marvel, a testament to the universe's vast power.

Salty Secrets: Unlike most crater lakes, Lonar is saline, with a unique ecosystem thriving within its waters. Spot diverse birdlife, including migratory flamingos, and marvel at the unusual presence of algae and bacteria adapted to its salty depths.

Legends and Myths: Local folklore weaves tales of the lake's origins, connecting it to gods and mythical creatures. Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of the region and discover the stories whispered across generations around this enigmatic wonder.

Gomukhtirtha

Sacred Spring: This natural spring, believed to be formed from the mouth of a mythical cow (Gomukh), holds immense religious significance for Hindus. Witness devotees bathe in its waters, seeking blessings and purification.

Medicinal Musing: Legend attributes healing properties to the spring's water, drawing visitors seeking cures for various ailments. While scientific evidence remains inconclusive, the belief in its restorative powers endures.

Tranquil Haven: Nestled amidst verdant hills, Gomukhtirtha offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle. Breathe in the fresh air, listen to the cascading water, and find solace in the peaceful ambiance of this sacred site.

Star-Gazing Paradise

Untainted Canvas: Escape the city lights and pollution to witness a breathtaking spectacle of stars like never before. Witness constellations dance across the inky expanse, spot shooting stars with naked eyes, and feel the awe-inspiring vastness of the universe engulf you.

Celestial Delights: Explore the wonders of the cosmos through astronomical events like meteor showers, eclipses, and planetary alignments. Join guided stargazing sessions, learn about constellations and myths, and deepen your understanding of the celestial ballet above.

Inner Connection: In the vastness of the night sky, find a sense of your own place in the universe. Stargazing offers an opportunity for introspection, a chance to ponder our existence and reconnect with the cosmic dance of life.
