Amruteshwar Temple, Ratanwadi

Amruteshwar Temple, Ratanwadi

  • Location: Ratanwadi village, near Bhandardara, Maharashtra
  • How to get there: The temple is about 17 km from Bhandardara and can be reached by road. The road is not in good condition, so it is best to take a taxi or jeep.
  • Opening hours: The temple is open 24 hours a day.
  • Entry fee: There is no entry fee.
  • Things to see: The main attraction of the temple is the sanctum, which houses a Shiva lingam. The walls and ceiling of the temple are decorated with beautiful murals and sculptures. There is also a mantapa with 12 pillars, each of which is carved with intricate designs.
  • Best time to visit: The best time to visit the temple is during the monsoon season, when the surrounding area is lush and green. However, the temple can be visited at any time of year.

    Kopeshwar Temple, Khidrapur

  • Location: Khidrapur village, Kolhapur district, Maharashtra
  • Age: 12th century, built by the Shilahara king Gandaraditya (1109-1178 CE)
  • Dedication: Lord Shiva, known as "Kopeshwar", meaning "Angry Shiva"
  • Unique Features: Blend of Hindu and Islamic architectural styles (due to Mughal influence) Exquisite carvings depicting the entire "Shivaleelamrit" mythological story No Nandi bull statue; separate Mandir dedicated to Nandi Statue of Vishnu (Dhopeshwar) and the Shivling "Kopeshwar" facing north within the sanctum sanctorum

    Daityasudana Temple, Lonar

  • Location: Lonar town, Buldhana
  • How to get there: Lonar is about 50 km from Aurangabad and can be reached by road. The temple is located in the center of the town.
  • Opening hours: The temple is open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
  • Entry fee: There is no entry fee.
  • Things to see: The main attraction of the temple is the sanctum sanctorum, which houses a statue of Lord Vishnu. The walls and ceiling of the temple are decorated with beautiful murals and sculptures. There is also a mandapa with 12 pillars, each of which is carved with intricate designs.
  • Best time to visit: The best time to visit the temple is during the monsoon season, when the surrounding area is lush and green. However, the temple can be visited at any time of year.

    Gondeshwar Temple, Sinnar

  • Location: Sinnar town, Nashik district, Maharashtra
  • How to get there: Sinnar is about 27 km from Nashik and can be reached by road. The temple is located on the outskirts of the town.
  • Opening hours: The temple is open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
  • Entry fee: There is no entry fee.
  • Things to see: The main attraction of the temple is the intricate carvings on the exterior and interior walls.
  • Best time to visit: The best time to visit the temple is during the monsoon season, when the surrounding area is lush and green. However, the temple can be visited at any time of year.
